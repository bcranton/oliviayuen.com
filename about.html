About Olivia


I am a visual artist and art educator based in Phoenix, Arizona. My recent watercolor paintings and linocut prints focus on plants and botanical subject matter, as well as explorations of my unique biracial heritage. Besides my personal creative endeavors, I regularly participate and sell my work in local events and markets. As co-founder of Wild Leaves PHX, my work has appeared in pop-ups at the Grand Avenue Festival, Palabras Bilingual Bookstore, Totem Yoga Studio, and the Melrose Vintage Market.

Follow along with my artistic process and daily life on Instagram @oliviameigan and @wildleaves.phx.
